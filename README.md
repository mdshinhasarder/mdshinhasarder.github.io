## 📖 Biography

**MD. Shinha Sarder** is a writer, YouTuber, and content creator born on **5 November 2004**. He shares creative content on YouTube, Facebook, and other platforms. He is currently studying **Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)** at **Northern University of Business and Technology, Khulna**.

He previously studied at **Khulna Zilla School**. His father, **MD. Lutfor Rahaman**, is a lawyer, and his mother, **Samima Sultana**, works in the private sector. He was born in **Shirgati village, Aichgati Union Parishad, Khulna**, and follows Islam.
